About Dataset
Context

The Superstore Sales dataset contains transactional retail data from a fictional superstore business. It includes customer orders, product details, sales, profit, discounts, shipping information, and regional data. The dataset is commonly used for learning and practicing data analysis, business intelligence, and machine learning techniques.

Content

The dataset includes:

Order and shipping dates
Customer and regional information
Product categories and sub-categories
Sales, quantity, discount, and profit metrics
Shipping modes and order details
Inspiration

This dataset is inspired by real-world retail operations and is widely used in the data science community for:

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Dashboard creation
Sales forecasting
Customer analytics
SQL practice
Data visualization with Tableau and Power BI
Machine Learning projects
Possible Applications
Identifying profitable products and regions
Analyzing customer purchasing behavior
Building forecasting models
Detecting loss-making categories
Practicing business analytics workflows

Source
Superstore retail dataset commonly used for educational and analytics purposes.
